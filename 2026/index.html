About the Conference

A premier global forum for intelligent & connected systems

The International Conference on Recent Trends in Machine Learning, IoT, Smart Cities & Applications (ICMISC 2026) returns for its 7th edition to bring together leading researchers, academicians, industry experts and research scholars from across the world.

The conference focuses on how intelligent machines and smart-city automation — powered by IoT sensors and artificial intelligence — can improve system efficiency across traffic management, power, water, waste, healthcare, education and community services, while addressing the privacy and security concerns of connected device networks.

Delegates present original, unpublished research through experimental results, novel designs, case studies and discussions on emerging interdisciplinary trends.

  • Rigorous double-blind peer review by an international panel
  • Proceedings publication — approval in progress (Springer-published & Scopus-indexed since 2020)
  • Distinguished international keynote speakers
  • Submissions via Microsoft CMT review platform
